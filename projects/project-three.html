Talk More

  • Year: 2025
  • Type: Campaign
  • Tools: Indesign
  • Context: Self-directed mini campaign.

Project Statement

'Talk More' is a 3-poster series that urges the public to communicate and interact with their community and neighbours more. Simply 'talk more' to neighbours, strangers, and whoever elses whose path you may cross. Using clean and minimal lines and shapes, placing emphasis on human connection and intimacy through space and closeness.

Reflection

As a self-directed mini-project, it was fun to think about what message I wanted to convey and through what images and captions. Coming up with the poster texts was a fun process of writing and rewriting; in efforts to choose statements that were direct and introspective. The colour combos seen on the posters were chosen due to their vibrant and eye-catching nature.

Role: Designer, Writer
