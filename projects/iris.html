Iris

  • Year: 2025
  • Type: Logo
  • Tools: Illustrator
  • Context: Self-directed logo making.

Project Statement

'Iris' logo branding for casual eyewear brand. Humourous and light-hearted, as evidenced by the image created when logo is rotated. The logo was first designed to fit within a circular shape; the face was found later once editing. 'Iris' is a reference to the coloured part of the eye that controls pupil size.

Reflection

Enjoyable practice and made for an interesting business idea; centred on human interaction.
