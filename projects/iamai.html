I.AM.AI

  • Year: 2025
  • Type: Campaign
  • Tools: Indesign, Illustrator
  • Context: University led project with intructions to design and direct a social campaign with the intention to motivate and incite community change.

Project Statement

The goals of this campaign include:

  • Inform, educate and raise awareness in relation to the environmental consequences of Artifical Intelligience.
  • Encourage discussion and action in response to how AI has changed our lives, for better and for worse.
  • Motivate a prioritisation of community connections and relationships, as opposed to emotional connection to AI.

    • Reflection

    Using illustrator to design and create the vector images that would be used in the campaign was a learning experience, especially after printing drafts and being able to see how well they translated in real-life applications. Having established a vibrant, clear and impactful visual language for the campaign, I found it relatively straightfoward to apply messaging and intent across different mediums of public messages.
